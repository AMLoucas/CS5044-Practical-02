Football Transfer Market Analytics

Analytics on how the transfer market has changed between the top 5 leagues in the past seasons

About Transfer Market Analytics


The football transfer market has transformed and changed over the years. With the help of visualisation we will be exploring the following question:


• How do the top 5 league transfer spending’s compare with each other over the seasons and what players positions are mostly involved in these transfers?


Together with the visualisation techniques we will use data obtained from Kaggle. The dataset contains information on the 250 most expensive transfers from each past season. From this data we filtered out and obtained only data that concerns the top 5 football leagues.


You can visit the 'Analytics' tab to start exploring. For the tutorial you can preview the video provided, which provides instructions on how the different visuals are used to obtain insights. In addition to this the report is an extra manual on how the visuals are used.
